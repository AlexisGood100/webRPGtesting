Instructions

You must first go to the map and click on one of the world tiles. This will bring you into a battle.

In this battle, you can use Attack, Defend, 3 types of special attack, use a mana potion, a healing potion, or switch to your familiar.

Through battle you will earn gold that you can use to start production, buy weapons and armor, or use to buy houses.

You can use the buy items from the merchant, which will go into your inventory.

From the inventory, you can choose to equip items. You can go to the equip tab to see equipped items and unequip them.

You will gain levels and your attributes will increase with each battle.

To see these instructions again, you can hit the space bar at any time.
